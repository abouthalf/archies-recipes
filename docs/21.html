Dutch cake

  • 2 qt. flour
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 5 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbs butter

Mix all together then add:

  • ½ lb seeded raisins, floured
  • ½ lb currants
  • 1 qt milk

Mix all together and put in 3 loaf pans. Bake about 45 mins in 350 oven. Ice with a thin icing made by mixing conf. sugar, a little butter, and warm milk together.
