Hot rolls

  • 1 qt sweet milk
  • 2 yeast cakes
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup lard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup lard

Add lard and sugar to milk and bring to a boiling point, let cool. Take 1 cup mixture and add 2 yeast cakes then add 5 cups flour mix and let stand until it rises twice its size. Add 6 cups flour. Put in ice box, use when needed.

Hot rolls

  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons lard
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 yeast cake
  • 9 cups flour or enough to make it leave pan.

Mix sugar, salt, lard and pour hot milk over this until dissolved. Let cool until lukewarm. Dissolve yeast cake in a little cold water then add to first mixture then add flour and beat until it bubbles. Then add enough flour to make dough. Let rise 3 times then bake.

Hot rolls

  • 5 cups flour sifted with 1 teaspoon soda
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 12 tablespoons shortening blended with dry ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups sour milk into which dissolve 1 yeast cake.

Set in ice box over nice. Cut out and let rise 2 1/2 hours before baking in 500ºF oven.
