Hot tamale pie

  • 1 cup corn meal
  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ chili powder

Make a mash of cornmeal and water. Fry onions in butter until well browned. Add chili powder, then meat, and when brown, add tomato sauce. Spread the mush in the bottom of a baking pan, saving some to spread over the top. Pour in meat mixture, then remaining mush. Bake in moderate oven 30 minutes.

Rolled and stuffed meat loaf

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper

Stuffing

  • ½ lb. ground pork
  • 1 medium potato, grated
  • ¼ cup fine chopped onion
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 ½ cup soft bread crumbs
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoon poultry seasoning

Combine all ingredients. Mix thoroughly, shape on wax paper into a rectangle about 10 x 16 in. Spread stuffing evenly over meat. Roll as a jelly roll and place seam side down on rack in open roasting pan. Bake at 350 degrees 1 ½ hours.
