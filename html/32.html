Puddings

Lemon meringue bread pudding

  • 3 eggs separated
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 cup milk
  • Juice 1 ½ lemon
  • 10 slices bread, cubed
  • Grate rind of 1 lemon
  • ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 6 tablespoons powdered sugar

Arrange bread in buttered casserole. Beat egg yolks with sugar until light. Add lemon rind and juice and nutmeg. Stir in milk, pour over bread. Bake in a moderate oven (350ºF) about 30 minutes. Make a meringue with egg whites and powdered sugar, and spread over pudding. Return to oven until lightly browned.

Creamy rice pudding

  • ½ cup rice
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs well beaten
  • ¾ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Wash rice, add water and salt, and cook, covered, in a double boiler about 40 minutes, until rice is tender. Add milk, eggs, and raisins. Flavor with nutmeg and vanilla. Cook 10 minutes longer.

Chocolate tapioca pudding

Mix together:

  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ tablespoon butter
  • ¼ cup tapioca
  • ½ cup cocoa
  • 1 ½ cup boiling water
  • 1 cup PET milk chilled and chipped

Flavor with 1 ½ teaspoon vanilla. Add to cooked mixture and serve.
