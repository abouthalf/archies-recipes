Nut bread

  • 3 cups sifted flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Sift all together

Then add 1 cup chopped pecans 1 cup raisins (white ones preferred but the dark ones are ok.) Mix these well in dry ingredients and then add 1 cup sweet milk and 1 egg. Bake in a loaf pan about 1 hour slowly about 275ºF or 300ºF

  • Mrs. W.J. Roe

Gingerbread

  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Mix in a bowl 1 egg beaten lightly:

  • 5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup dark molasses
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Add flour mixture to bowl, stirring steadily. When well mixed, beat hard for 2 minutes. Turn into 8 inch greased gingerbread pan. Bake in 350ºF oven for 30 minutes.
