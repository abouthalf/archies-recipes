Ice box hot rolls

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 qt sweet milk
  • 6 or 7 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon soda
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 cake yeast
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Put milk, sugar, and shortening in and let come to boil. Cool to lukewarm. Dissolve yeast in 1/2 cup warm water, add to first mixture, add 5 cups flour. Let rise 2 hours, then take 2 cups flour and add soda, salt, and baking powder and sift together, add first mixture. Don’t work to much. Place in greased bowl and put in ice box 2 hours before baking and let it rise 2 hours. 400ยบ 12 to 15 min.
