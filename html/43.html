Frostings or Icings

Layer cake #1 (Mrs. Lawrence)

  • 2 cups sugar
  • ¼ cup cocoa
  • 1 cup Karo syrup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 lump butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Coconut Cream #2 (Cocoa cream)

  • 2 tablespoons Crisco
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup cocoa
  • 2 ½ cup sifted confectioner’s sugar
  • about 6 tablespoons scalded cream

Blend Crisco, butter, vanilla, and salt. Sift cocoa with sugar. Beat in ½ cup (sugar and coca mixture) add hot cream, alternately with remaining (sugar and cocoa mixture) beating well after each addition. Add only enough cream to make a nice spreading consistency.

Spice cake #3

  • ⅓ cup Crisco
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup chopped raisins
  • 2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 ⅓ cup sifted B. sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 ½ teaspoon B.P.
  • ½ teaspoons cinnamon

Cake

Put all ingredients together in mixing bowl and beat thoroughly until smooth. Bake 20 minutes, in layer cake tins at (370ºF)

Frosting
