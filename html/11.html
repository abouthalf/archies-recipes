Cocoa cream cake

  • 2/3 cup Crisco
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs unbeaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon B.P.
  • 1/2 teaspoon soda
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 cup sour cream

Blend crisco, salt and vanilla. Add sugar gradually and cream well. Add eggs singly, beating well after each addition. Sift baking powder, soda, and cocoa with flour 3 times. Add flour to creamed mixture alternately with sour cream, mixing after each addition until smooth.

Bake in 2 8 inch greased layer pans in moderate overn (350ยบ F) 30 to 35 minutes.

Spread cocoa cream frosting between layers and on top and sides of cake.
