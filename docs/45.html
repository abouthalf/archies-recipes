(Chocolate) #7 (Mrs Keith)

  • 2 cups B. sugar
  • ½ cups milk
  • ½ cup butter
  • Chocolate to color

Cook until thick, spread on cake while warm

Caramel #8, for Irish potato cake

  • 2 cup B. sugar
  • 1 cup w. sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup hot water

Boil sugar and water together until it ropes. Add milk and butter and cook 5 or 10 minutes longer.

#9 (Mrs Ramsauer) (pineapple filling)

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • ½ lemon
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 10¢ can pineapple
  • 1 can coconut

Cook in double boiler thoroughly.

Lemon cheese filling

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour or cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon B.P.
  • Juice 2 lemons
  • Rind 2 lemons

Cook in double boiler until thick.

Chocolate syrup

  • 1 cup w sugar
  • 1 cup b sugar
  • 1 cup water or milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 2 tablespoons flour or cornstarch
