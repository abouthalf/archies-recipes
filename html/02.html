Beverages

Chilled fruit punch

  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 2 cups iced tea

Boil sugar and water 1 minute, cool and add rest of ingredients. Serve in glasses 1/3 filled with chopped ice.

Egg nog

  • 1 egg
  • pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • milk
  • 2 tablespoons fruit juice

Add salt to egg white and beat to a stiff froth. Add sugar, the well beaten yolk and fruit juice. Fill the glass with cold milk, sprinkle the top with chopped nuts. The whole egg may be beaten instead of separately if desired.

Russian tea

  • 6 teaspoons tea
  • 2 teaspoons cloves
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 4 qts water
  • 4 lemons
  • 1/2 doz oranges

Put tea and spices in little cloth and boil. Add sugar and fruit juices.
