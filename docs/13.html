Angel food cake

  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 1 1/2 cup sifted sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon C. tartar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Add 1/2 the sugar to the flour and sift 4 times. Beat eggs and salt till foaming and add C. tartar and beat till stiff but not dry. Add the remaining sugar then sprinkle flour over little at a time till all is used. Add flavor. Bake in oven 325 ºF 1 hour or until done.

Pound cake (Mrs. Logan)

  • 10 eggs
  • 2 cups soft butter
  • 3 1/2 cups sugar
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon lemon
  • pinch salt

Cream butter and sugar real good and beat in eggs one at a time. Don’t use any B.P. or milk or water. 2 hours 250 ºF.
