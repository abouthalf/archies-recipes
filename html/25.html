Cookies

Drop cookies

  • 1.2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ½ cup flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoon B.P.
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed tight
  • 1 cup nuts
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup Grape Nut flakes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • pinch salt

Ice box cookies

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 ½ cup Crisco
  • 3 eggs
  • 5 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup nuts

Make 3 rolls and roll in wax paper and put in icebox.

— Mrs. Ranisour

Orange tea cookies

  • ½ cup butter
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • ⅓ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon each of orange, lemon rind grated

Glaze top with egg white. Put nots on top. Bake in oven (350ºF)

Filled cookies

  • ½ cup Crisco
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons B.P.
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Filling for cookies

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup raisins or figs chopped fine
  • 1 tablespoon flour

Cook until thick and cool.
