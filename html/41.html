Cherry cobbler (Grace Ooter)

  • ⅔ stick Oleo, melt in Pyrex dish

Sift together:

  • 1 c. sugar
  • 1 c. flour
  • 2 ½ teaspoon B.P.
  • pinch salt

Pour ¾ cup milk over this mix then pour over butter. Empty 1 can sour cherries into dish. Sprinkle a little sugar over cherries. Dash of cinnamon. Bake 30 to 40 min, 350ºF. Fruit goest to bottom, crust comest to top. I also use apples the same way.
